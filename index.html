Hi there! I'm Devin and I make different things to put online. Most of them are games and art, thanks for checking them out! If you want to reach out to me, I can best be contacted at dmbeck9459@gmail.com :)

I made a game called Find The Spirits on Roblox! I made the base game during a month of hyperfixation in the summer of 2024, and it did better than I ever expected it to. I still like to work on it every now and then!

In the summer of 2023, I made Shoecake as an entry to an Adventure GameJam contest. It's a simple text-based story about love, loss, and memory. It is also about caked shoes and shoe cakes.

Flipnote animations of incredibly high quality. Nothing is funnier than audio recorded on a 3ds. I made quite a few of these in rapid succession and it was the first time I'd had something I put online do well!

Panicked Pizzas is a fast-paced pizza baking game where a new state-of-the-art ingredient-blasting system has been invented. There's different difficulties and achievements to unlock!

Interstellar Outlaw was the first game I ever finished. It has a bunch of issues but I think it's still fun :] You can unlock different UFOs and some of them are weird like a dinosaur UFO or a waffle UFO!!!!!

I never completed it, but Confection Resurrection was going to be a game about working Death's new bakery and serving the denizens of the ethereal plane. It was clunky and full of issues, and I learned what feature creep was for the first time....
